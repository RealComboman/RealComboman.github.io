Game Rules

JewelCrush is a puzzle game that takes the form of a grid of 8 squares out of 8 containing stones of different colors. The goal of the game is to group them by color in groups of 3 or more, horizontally or vertically, inverting two adjacent gems each time.

When they are aligned, they disappear, and the ones above them fall, revealing new gems. You will earn bonus points if you manage to align more than 3 identical stones. As the gems fall, it's possible for them to align, causing a chain reaction that will also earn you bonus points. The more chains you create in a row, the more points you'll earn.

After a certain number of chains, you will be able to see a bonus object appear on your grid, a candy bomb which will allow you to destroy all the surrounding gems.


You have 3 minutes to reach the required number of points at each level.

You will have lost the game if you fail to complete the level within the allotted time, or if no more combinations are possible on your grid.

Need help? If you get stuck on a level, don't panic, you'll be given a hint after a certain amount of inactivity.
